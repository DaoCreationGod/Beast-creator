African Bush Elephant (Loxodonta africana) - Trunk, tusks, wrinkled skin.
White Rhinoceros (Ceratotherium simum) - Square lips, double horn.
Hippopotamus (Hippopotamus amphibius) - Barrel-shaped, gaping jaws.
Woolly Mammoth (Mammuthus primigenius†) - Shaggy fur, curved tusks.
Paraceratherium (Paraceratherium transouralicum†) - Tallest land mammal ever, giraffe-like neck.
Indian Elephant (Elephas maximus) - Smaller ears, domed head.
Steppe Bison (Bison priscus†) - Enormous horns, humped shoulders.
Giant Ground Sloth (Megatherium americanum†) - Hooked claws, towering posture.
Deinotherium (Deinotherium giganteum†) - Downward-curving tusks.
Cape Buffalo (Syncerus caffer) - Bossed horns, bulky frame.
Glyptodon (Glyptodon clavipes†) - Armored carapace, club tail.
Bactrian Camel (Camelus bactrianus) - Two humps, shaggy winter coat.
Moose (Alces alces) - Palmate antlers, dewlap.
Chalicotherium (Chalicotherium goldfussi†) - Gorilla-like posture, clawed feet.
Uintatherium (Uintatherium anceps†) - Six skull horns, saber teeth.
Arsinoitherium (Arsinoitherium zitteli†) - Twin nasal horns, rhino-like build.
Megacerops (Megacerops coloradensis†) - Y-shaped horn, titanothere.
Elasmotherium (Elasmotherium sibiricum†) - “Siberian Unicorn,” single massive horn.
Forest Elephant (Loxodonta cyclotis) - Straight tusks, smaller size.
Pygmy Hippopotamus (Choeropsis liberiensis) - Compact, less aquatic.
Columbian Mammoth (Mammuthus columbi†) - Less hairy, North American.
Mastodon (Mammut americanum†) - Cone-shaped teeth, stocky build.
Toxodon (Toxodon platensis†) - Hippo-like, rhino-sized.
Giant Wombat (Diprotodon optatum†) - Marsupial “rhino,” pouch.
Embolotherium (Embolotherium andrewsi†) - Battering-ram skull crest.
