Hippopotamus (Hippopotamus amphibius) - Barrel-shaped, gaping jaws.
Platypus (Ornithorhynchus anatinus) - Duckbill, webbed feet, venomous spur.
Capybara (Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris) - Rodent-like, blunt snout, partially webbed toes.
Beaver (Castor canadensis) - Flat tail, chisel teeth, waterproof fur.
Amazonian Manatee (Trichechus inunguis) - Paddle-shaped flippers, whiskered face.
Water Opossum (Chironectes minimus) - Webbed hind feet, marsupial pouch.
Muskrat (Ondatra zibethicus) - Rat-like, laterally flattened tail.
Pygmy Hippopotamus (Choeropsis liberiensis) - Compact, less aquatic than its cousin.
Desmostylus (Desmostylus hesperus†) - Extinct “sea camel,” columnar teeth.
Sea Otter (Enhydra lutris) - Dense fur, floating posture.
Ambulocetus (Ambulocetus natans†) - “Walking whale,” crocodile-like body.
Mink (Neovison vison) - Sleek, semi-webbed feet.
Yapok (Chironectes minimus) - Aquatic marsupial with waterproof pouch.
European Otter (Lutra lutra) - Streamlined, tapered tail.
Dugong (Dugong dugon) - Rounded flippers, whale-like tail.
Water Chevrotain (Hyemoschus aquaticus) - Tiny deer-like, dappled coat.
Giant Otter (Pteronura brasiliensis) - Snake-like neck, webbed paws.
Semi-Aquatic Dinosaur (Spinosaurus aegyptiacus†) - Crocodile snout, sailback.
Marsh Deer (Blastocerus dichotomus) - Long legs, splayed hooves.
Marine Iguana (Amblyrhynchus cristatus) - Salt-sneezing, black scales.
Water Shrew (Neomys fodiens) - Venomous saliva, fringed feet.
Desert Warthog (Phacochoerus aethiopicus) - Sparse fur, mud-wallowing.
Elephant Seal (Mirounga spp.) - Bulbous proboscis, massive size.
Thalassocnus (Thalassocnus natans†) - Sloth adapted for grazing seagrass.
Chinese Giant Salamander (Andrias davidianus) - Wrinkled skin, fully aquatic amphibian.
