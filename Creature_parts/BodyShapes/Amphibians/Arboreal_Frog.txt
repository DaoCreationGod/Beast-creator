Red-Eyed Tree Frog (Agalychnis callidryas) - Neon green, bulging red eyes.
Glass Frog (Hyalinobatrachium spp.) - Transparent belly, visible organs.
White’s Tree Frog (Ranoidea caerulea) - Plump, blue-green.
Gliding Tree Frog (Agalychnis spurrelli) - Webbed feet for aerial glides.
Amazon Milk Frog (Trachycephalus resinifictrix) - Milky secretions, blue-speckled.
Poison Dart Frog (Dendrobates tinctorius) - Aposematic colors, toxic.
Waxy Monkey Frog (Phyllomedusa sauvagii) - Waxy skin, leaf-nesting.
Flying Frog (Rhacophorus nigropalmatus) - Wing-like webbed feet.
Mossy Frog (Theloderma corticale) - Camouflaged like moss.
Climbing Salamander (Bolitoglossa spp.) - Prehensile tail, tropical.
Malayan Horned Frog (Megophrys nasuta) - Leaf-mimicking, pointy "horns."
Vietnamese Mossy Frog (Theloderma corticale) - Bumpy, moss-like texture.
Blue Poison Dart Frog (Dendrobates tinctorius "azureus") - Cobalt blue, black spots.
Tomato Frog (Dyscophus antongilii) - Bright red, inflates when threatened.
Wallace’s Flying Frog (Rhacophorus nigropalmatus) - Glides between trees.
Golden Poison Frog (Phyllobates terribilis) - Most toxic vertebrate.
Harlequin Tree Frog (Rhacophorus pardalis) - Flattened body, glider.
Green-and-Black Poison Frog (Dendrobates auratus) - Metallic sheen.
Bumblebee Poison Frog (Dendrobates leucomelas) - Yellow/black stripes.
Lemur Leaf Frog (Agalychnis lemur) - Slender, vertical pupils.
Clown Tree Frog (Dendropsophus leucophyllatus) - Striped, nocturnal.
Black-Eyed Tree Frog (Agalychnis moreletii) - Black eyes, lime-green body.
Hourglass Tree Frog (Dendropsophus ebraccatus) - Hourglass-shaped markings.
Splash-Backed Poison Frog (Adelphobates galactonotus) - Milky-blue back.
Spotted Tree Frog (Nyctixalus pictus) - White-spotted, arboreal.
