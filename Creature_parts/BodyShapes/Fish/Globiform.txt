Ocean Sunfish (Mola mola) - Flattened disc, largest bony fish.
Pufferfish (Tetraodontidae spp.) - Inflatable, spiky skin.
Porcupinefish (Diodon hystrix) - Spines erect when inflated.
Boxfish (Ostracion cubicus) - Cubic armor, yellow with black spots.
Cowfish (Lactoria cornuta) - Horn-like projections.
Humphead Parrotfish (Bolbometopon muricatum) - Bulbous forehead.
Blobfish (Psychrolutes marcidus) - Gelatinous deep-sea dweller.
Balloonfish (Diodon holocanthus) - White spots, inflatable.
Stonefish (Synanceia verrucosa) - Camouflaged, venomous spines.
Frogfish (Antennarius spp.) - Warty, lure-equipped.
Pineapplefish (Cleidopus gloriamaris) - Glowing scales, armored plates.
Coffinfish (Chaunacidae spp.) - Pink, blob-like deep-sea fish.
Hairy Frogfish (Antennarius striatus) - Filamentous “hair.”
Lumpsucker (Cyclopterus lumpus) - Suction-cup pelvic fins.
Toadfish (Opsanus tau) - Wide mouth, mottled skin.
Stargazer (Uranoscopus sulphureus) - Upward-facing eyes, buried predator.
Swallowtail Angelfish (Genicanthus melanospilos) - Round-bodied, lyre tail.
Barrelfish (Hyperoglyphe antarctica) - Deep-bodied, metallic sheen.
Giant Gourami (Osphronemus goramy) - Air-breathing, rounded profile.
Pygmy Seahorse (Hippocampus bargibanti) - Coral-mimicking, tiny.
Bumphead Parrotfish (Bolbometopon muricatum) - Bulldozer-like forehead.
Red Lionfish (Pterois volitans) - Flamboyant spines, striped.
Anglerfish (Melanocetus johnsonii) - Bioluminescent lure, globular body.
Crested Oysterfish (Encheliophis vermicularis) - Eel-like, pearlfish mimic.
Whale Shark (Rhincodon typus) - Massive filter-feeder, spotted.
