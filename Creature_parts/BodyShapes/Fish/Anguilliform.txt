Moray Eel (Muraena helena) - Jagged teeth, lurks in crevices.
Electric Eel (Electrophorus electricus) - Shocks prey, air-breathing.
Gulper Eel (Eurypharynx pelecanoides) - Huge mouth, deep-sea.
Ribbon Eel (Rhinomuraena quaesita) - Blue/yellow, flared nostrils.
Conger Eel (Conger conger) - Massive, nocturnal.
American Eel (Anguilla rostrata) - Catadromous migration.
Oarfish (Regalecus glesne) - Silvery, serpentine, longest bony fish.
Snake Eel (Ophichthidae spp.) - Burrowing, pointed tail.
Snipe Eel (Nemichthys scolopaceus) - Needle-like jaws.
Cutthroat Eel (Synaphobranchus spp.) - Deep-sea scavenger.
Hagfish (Myxine glutinosa) - Slime-producing, jawless.
Lamprey (Petromyzon marinus) - Suction-cup mouth, parasitic.
Dragon Eel (Enchelycore pardalis) - Jagged teeth, leopard spots.
Spaghetti Eel (Moringua spp.) - Thread-like, burrowing.
Pelican Eel (Eurypharynx pelecanoides) - Expandable jaw.
Wolf Eel (Anarrhichthys ocellatus) - Crushing teeth, wolf-like.
Garden Eel (Heterocongrinae spp.) - Colonies in sandy burrows.
Freshwater Eel (Anguilla anguilla) - Catadromous, snake-like.
Sawtooth Eel (Serrivomeridae spp.) - Serrated teeth.
Slender Snipe Eel (Nemichthys scolopaceus) - Whip-like tail.
Tiger Moray (Gymnothorax polyuranodon) - Striped, aggressive.
Zombie Worm (Osedax spp.) - Bone-eating, not a true eel.
Deepsea Spiny Eel (Notacanthidae spp.) - Spiny back, benthic.
Saccopharyngiform (Saccopharynx ampullaceus) - Gulper eel relative.
Leptocephalus (Leptocephalus spp.) - Transparent eel larvae.
