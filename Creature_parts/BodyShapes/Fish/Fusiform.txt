Atlantic Bluefin Tuna (Thunnus thynnus) - Metallic blue, crescent tail.
Swordfish (Xiphias gladius) - Elongated bill, sleek body.
Great Barracuda (Sphyraena barracuda) - Silver with dark bars, razor teeth.
Mako Shark (Isurus oxyrinchus) - Lunate tail, fastest shark.
Rainbow Trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss) - Pink lateral stripe, spotted.
Tarpon (Megalops atlanticus) - Silver scales, upturned jaw.
Wahoo (Acanthocybium solandri) - Blue stripes, razor teeth.
Bonito (Sarda sarda) - Striped back, bullet-shaped.
Leedsichthys (Leedsichthys problematicus†) - Extinct giant filter-feeder.
Bluefish (Pomatomus saltatrix) - Green-blue back, voracious predator.
Xiphactinus (Xiphactinus audax†) - Extinct “bulldog fish,” 20-foot predator.
Dorado (Coryphaena hippurus) - Golden-green, iridescent.
Yellowfin Tuna (Thunnus albacares) - Bright yellow fins.
Sockeye Salmon (Oncorhynchus nerka) - Red body during spawning.
Marlin (Istiophorus platypterus) - Sail-like dorsal fin.
Herring (Clupea harengus) - Silver schooling fish.
Megalodon (Otodus megalodon†) - Extinct giant shark, 60-foot predator.
Opah (Lampris guttatus) - Round, iridescent red fins.
Pike (Esox lucius) - Duckbill snout, olive-green body.
Houndfish (Tylosurus crocodilus) - Needle-like jaws.
Dunkleosteus (Dunkleosteus terrelli†) - Extinct armored predator.
Snook (Centropomus undecimalis) - Lateral line, protruding lower jaw.
Coho Salmon (Oncorhynchus kisutch) - Silver with red flank.
Crevalle Jack (Caranx hippos) - Brassy yellow, deep-bodied.
Sardine (Sardina pilchardus) - Small, oily, silver-blue.
