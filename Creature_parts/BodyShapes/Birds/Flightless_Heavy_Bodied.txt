Ostrich (Struthio camelus) - Long neck, two-toed feet.
Southern Cassowary (Casuarius casuarius) - Helmet-like casque, electric-blue neck.
Emperor Penguin (Aptenodytes forsteri) - Black-and-white tuxedo, huddling posture.
Kiwi (Apteryx mantelli) - Hair-like feathers, long beak.
Moa (Dinornis robustus†) - Extinct 12-foot-tall herbivore.
Dodo (Raphus cucullatus†) - Hooked beak, plump body.
Greater Rhea (Rhea americana) - Gray plumage, flightless speedster.
Kakapo (Strigops habroptilus) - Owl-like face, moss-green feathers.
Takahe (Porphyrio hochstetteri) - Iridescent blue-green plumage.
Elephant Bird (Aepyornis maximus†) - Largest bird ever, elephantine legs.
Adélie Penguin (Pygoscelis adeliae) - White eye-ring, tuxedo coloring.
Little Penguin (Eudyptula minor) - Blue-gray back, tiny stature.
Giant Moa (Dinornis novaezealandiae†) - Neck taller than giraffes.
Humboldt Penguin (Spheniscus humboldti) - Black horseshoe band on chest.
Terror Bird (Phorusrhacos longissimus†) - Extinct apex predator, hooked beak.
Emu (Dromaius novaehollandiae) - Shaggy brown feathers, three-toed feet.
Rockhopper Penguin (Eudyptes chrysocome) - Yellow crest, spiky feathers.
Great Auk (Pinguinus impennis†) - Black-and-white, penguin-like (Northern Hemisphere).
Rodrigues Solitaire (Pezophaps solitaria†) - Extinct, knobbed wings for combat.
King Penguin (Aptenodytes patagonicus) - Orange ear patches, regal stance.
Crested Moa (Pachyornis australis†) - Stout-legged, herbivorous giant.
Inaccessible Island Rail (Atlantisia rogersi) - Smallest flightless bird.
Magellanic Penguin (Spheniscus magellanicus) - Double black stripes on chest.
Moa-Nalo (Chelychelynechen quassus†) - Extinct Hawaiian duck-moa hybrid.
Waitaha Penguin (Megadyptes waitaha†) - Recently extinct, yellow-eyed.
