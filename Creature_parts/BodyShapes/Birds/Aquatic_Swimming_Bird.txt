Emperor Penguin (Aptenodytes forsteri) - Black-and-white tuxedo, deep diver.
Atlantic Puffin (Fratercula arctica) - Colorful beak, clown-like face.
Great Cormorant (Phalacrocorax carbo) - Hooked bill, wings spread to dry.
Common Loon (Gavia immer) - Red eyes, haunting call.
Mallard Duck (Anas platyrhynchos) - Iridescent green head, orange legs.
Horned Grebe (Podiceps auritus) - Golden "horns," red neck.
King Eider (Somateria spectabilis) - Bulbous orange bill, multicolored head.
Black Skimmer (Rynchops niger) - Lower mandible longer than upper.
Albatross (Diomedea exulans) - Longest wingspan, gliding mastery.
Great Auk (Pinguinus impennis†) - Extinct, penguin-like in the Arctic.
Red-Breasted Merganser (Mergus serrator) - Serrated bill, shaggy crest.
Tufted Duck (Aythya fuligula) - Drooping crest, golden eyes.
Northern Gannet (Morus bassanus) - Spear-diving, azure-tipped wings.
Hooded Merganser (Lophodytes cucullatus) - Fan-shaped crest, piercing eyes.
Arctic Tern (Sterna paradisaea) - Longest migration, blood-red bill.
Spectacled Eider (Somateria fischeri) - Green "goggles," velvety plumage.
Australasian Darter (Anhinga novaehollandiae) - Snake-like neck, spear fisher.
Razorbill (Alca torda) - White-striped bill, penguin-like stance.
Mandarin Duck (Aix galericulata) - Ornate, sail-shaped feathers.
Pelagornis (Pelagornis sandersi†) - Extinct giant with tooth-like beak projections.
Pink-Eared Duck (Malacorhynchus membranaceus) - Zebra-striped flanks, spatula bill.
Black-Footed Albatross (Phoebastria nigripes) - Sooty plumage, oceanic wanderer.
Crested Auklet (Aethia cristatella) - Whiskered face, citrus scent.
Red-Throated Loon (Gavia stellata) - Slender build, uptilted bill.
Magpie Goose (Anseranas semipalmata) - Partially webbed feet, pied plumage.
